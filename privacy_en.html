Privacy Policy

App: Pixel Fake  ·  Developed by: Cervena Software  ·  Last updated: June 2026

Plain-language summary: Pixel Fake analyzes images to detect whether they were AI-generated. Images you submit for analysis are never stored on our servers. We only collect the minimum data needed to run the app. If you choose not to share usage data, the app works exactly the same.

1. Who We Are

Pixel Fake is developed and operated by Cervena Software, based in Buenos Aires, Argentina. For any privacy-related questions, please contact us at cervenasoftware@gmail.com.

2. What Data We Collect and Why

2.1 Account Data

To create your account we need your email address and a password. This information is stored securely in Firebase Authentication (Google LLC) and is used exclusively to identify you and protect your account.

2.2 Subscription Data

Subscription and payment management is handled by Apple App Store, Google Play, or RevenueCat Inc. We receive confirmation of your subscription status (active, cancelled, etc.) but we never have access to your credit card or payment information. That data is handled exclusively by Apple, Google, or RevenueCat as applicable.

2.3 Images Analyzed

Images you submit for analysis are transmitted over an encrypted connection (HTTPS) to our backend, where they are processed by the Sightengine service (sightengine.com). Original images are not stored on any server. We store only a cryptographic hash (SHA-256) of the analysis result in cache for up to 30 days, in order to avoid processing the same image twice and to optimize app performance. This hash cannot be used to reconstruct the original image.

2.4 Analysis History

Your analysis history (score, date, small thumbnail) is saved locally on your device using secure on-device storage. We do not sync this history to any server. If you uninstall the app or delete your account, your local history is permanently removed.

2.5 Usage and Diagnostic Data (opt-in)

Only if you explicitly consent during onboarding or in Settings, we collect anonymous usage data (e.g., which features you use, how many analyses you perform per session) via Firebase Analytics, and error diagnostic data via Firebase Crashlytics. This data is fully anonymous and is used solely to improve the app. If you do not consent, we collect no usage or diagnostic data whatsoever. You can change this preference at any time in Settings → Privacy.

2.6 Basic Technical Data

For the app to function correctly, our backend logs minimal operational data such as: an anonymous user identifier (generated by Firebase), the number of analyses performed in the current month, and timestamps required for quota control and rate limiting. This data does not include any personally identifiable information beyond your account email.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide and maintain the AI-generated image detection service.
  • To manage your account, subscription, and free trial period.
  • To enforce fair use of the service (monthly analysis limit and rate limiting).
  • To respond to your support requests.
  • To improve the app through anonymous usage data, only if you have given consent.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for commercial or advertising purposes.

4. Third Parties With Access to Data

We use the following trusted service providers to operate the app:

  • Google Firebase (Firebase Authentication, Firestore, Cloud Functions, Analytics, Crashlytics) — account storage, backend infrastructure, and, if you consent, analytics and diagnostics. Privacy policy: policies.google.com/privacy
  • Sightengine — AI image analysis service. Images are sent to Sightengine for classification and are not retained beyond immediate processing. Privacy policy: sightengine.com/privacy
  • RevenueCat — subscription management. Privacy policy: revenuecat.com/privacy
  • Apple Inc. / Google LLC — payment processing and app distribution. Their privacy policies apply to transactions made through their respective stores.

5. Data Retention

  • Account data: for as long as your account is active.
  • Analysis cache (hash): up to 30 days from the last access.
  • Local history: until you delete it manually or uninstall the app.
  • Subscription data: as required by applicable legal and commercial obligations.
  • When you delete your account from the app, all your data on our servers is deleted within a maximum of 30 days.

6. Your Rights

In accordance with applicable privacy laws, including Argentina's Personal Data Protection Act (Law No. 25,326) and principles of the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correct inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Delete your account and all associated data (button available in Settings → My Account).
  • Export your data in JSON format (available in Settings → My Account).
  • Withdraw consent for analytics at any time in Settings → Privacy.
  • Object to the processing of your data by contacting us.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at cervenasoftware@gmail.com.

7. Security

We implement technical and organizational measures to protect your data: all communications use HTTPS/TLS encryption; API keys live exclusively in the backend (never in the app itself); data access is protected by Firebase Authentication and Firestore security rules; we use Firebase App Check to verify that requests originate from legitimate versions of the app.

8. Children's Privacy

Pixel Fake is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If you become aware that a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us so we can delete it.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through an in-app notice or by email. The date of the last update is always shown at the top of this document.
